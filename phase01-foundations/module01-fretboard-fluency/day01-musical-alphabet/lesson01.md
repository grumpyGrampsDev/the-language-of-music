# The Language of Music

> Phase 1 • Foundations
>
> Module 1 • Fretboard Fluency
>
> Day 1 • The Musical Alphabet

---

## Objective

Begin building a mental map of the guitar.

The goal is not to memorize fret numbers.

The goal is to begin seeing **notes**.

A guitarist thinks:

> Third fret.

A musician thinks:

> G.

---

## Lesson

There are only **12 notes**.

```
A
A#/Bb
B
C
C#/Db
D
D#/Eb
E
F
F#/Gb
G
G#/Ab
```

The pattern repeats forever.

### Important

There is no sharp between:

- B → C
- E → F

These are natural half steps.

---

## Exercise 1

Recite the musical alphabet:

- Forward
- Backward
- Starting from random notes

Examples:

Start on C:

```
C C# D D# E F F# G G# A A# B
```

Start on F:

```
F F# G G# A A# B C C# D D# E
```

## Exercise 2

3     G

4     G#

5     A

6     A#

7     B

8     C

9     C#

10    D

11    D#

Say every note aloud.

## Exercise 3

- Find G
- Find A
